CodeTelligence

Codetelligence is a Coding Bootcamp organization, aimed at equipping youth from disadvantaged communities with digital skills. We are active citizens that have an appreciation for technology and its impact to the global community. As such we have taken it upon ourselves to introduce software development skills and job-ready skills to mostly low income communities that experience some of the greatest challenges to adoption of technology. We are mainly focused on the North West and Western Cape provinces. We facilitate bootcamps for unemployed matriculants and workshops for teachers as they are charged with ensuring learners continue with applying newly acquired skills. We bridge the technology divide for impoverished communities as they are most impacted by lack of resources
